Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 29, 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Call Me Now ("the App") handles your information.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any personal information.

Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your phone number
  • Your contacts
  • Your location
  • Your messages
  • Your photos
  • Your call history
  • Your device identifiers
  • Analytics data
  • Usage statistics
  • Advertising identifiers

Contacts Permission

The App may request permission to access your contacts so you can select a contact name and photo to display during a simulated incoming call.

This access is used only on your device.

Your contacts are:

  • Never uploaded.
  • Never transmitted.
  • Never stored on our servers.
  • Never shared with any third party.

If you deny contact access, you can still use the App by manually entering a caller name if that feature is available.

Local Storage

The App may save certain preferences locally on your device, such as:

  • Selected contact
  • Preferred ringtone
  • Delay before simulated call
  • App settings

These settings remain entirely on your device and are never transmitted to us.

No Account Required

The App does not require registration or user accounts.

No Cloud Services

The App does not use cloud storage to store your information.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use third-party advertising networks or analytics services that collect personal information.

If future versions introduce optional third-party services, this Privacy Policy will be updated before those services are implemented.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed toward children under the age of 13 (or the applicable minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Security

Because we do not collect or store your personal information, there is no personal data maintained on our servers.

Any preferences stored by the App remain on your device and are protected by your device's operating system security features.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect or retain your personal information, there is no personal data for us to access, modify, export, or delete.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be published within the App listing or on our website, where applicable.

Contact

If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer using the support information provided on the App Store or Google Play listing.
